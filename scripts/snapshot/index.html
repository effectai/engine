Introduction

Effect AI will migrate it's ecosystem to Solana, powered by the EFFECT token. The new token will be automatically distributed to EFX holders on EOS and BSC via a claim portal. The portal will be made available on the distribution date.

This document describes the rules that are applied for the creation of the Snapshot and the distribution of the $EFFECT token.

After the snapshot date, EFFECT is the official Effect Network token.

  • Total Supply: 530,000,000 EFFECT
  • Token Type: Solana SPL
  • Decimals: 6

EFX Holders (EOS & BSC)

EFFECT tokens are distributed 1:1 to EFX holders.

EOS has an advanced account system. A single EOS account can have multiple key pairs associated with it. In 97% of the cases, the EFFECT claim portal can reliably map the first key in the account's active permission to the claim. In the remaining cases claim portal will still accomodate a migration.

Binance Smart Chain maps every normal account to a single key pair. This means that all tokens on BSC can be easily claimed in the portal.

The following rules are followed in the creation of the snapshot. Some are migrated in an alternative way.

1. Special accounts

The following special accounts are not included:

  • x.ptokens
  • bitbnsglobal
  • kucoinrise11
  • stakepool.efx
  • therealforce
  • efx
  • efxliquidity
  • xeth.ptokens
  • deposit.pro
  • withdraw.pro
  • efxstakepool
  • treasury.efx
  • bsc.efx
  • daoproposals
  • realeffectai
2. Low balances

Accounts with less than 5 EFX will not be included in the snapshot. This threshold avoids a lot of complicated account structures and experimental accounts that are hard to migrate. To include a low EFX balance in the migration it is necessary to acquire an EFX balance of 5 or higher.

3. Large balances

The migrations is capped at 20M $EFFECT tokens per user. At the time of writing no accounts come close to this limit.

4. Exchanges

EFX tokens stored on centralized exchanges are not included in the snapshot.

5. Liquidity Pools

Liquidity Providers have to remove their liquidity before the snapshot. Although the EFX balance of LP providers will be included in the snapshot at a best effort basis, EFX loses its main utility at the snapshot, which means the quote asset of the liquidity will get lost.
